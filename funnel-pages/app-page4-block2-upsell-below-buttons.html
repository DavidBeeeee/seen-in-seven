WHAT WORKING TOGETHER ACTUALLY FEELS LIKE

You have a direct line to David from noon to midnight, and I mean David, not a support team, not a chatbot, and not a weekly office hour you have to schedule your life around. You message him about your content, your positioning, your hooks, your confidence on camera, whatever is genuinely getting in your way, and he responds with the kind of specific and honest feedback that only comes from someone who's actually paying close attention to your particular situation.

Every month you're building something that compounds. Not just posting for the sake of having something on your feed, but developing a real presence that people recognize, creating content that connects with the right people, and building the kind of consistency that took other creators years to stumble into on their own through trial and error.

This program has a cap because genuine, hands-on support has a real ceiling. David works with a small number of clients at any given time, and if there's an opening when you're reading this, it's because someone recently wrapped up their engagement or the spot simply hasn't been filled yet. That changes without any advance notice.

No contracts. No commitment beyond the current month. If it stops working for you, you cancel and you part ways like adults who respect each other's time.
