Your 7 Video Challenge

Here's how the challenge works.

1
7 videos. Your timeline.
You don't have to post all 7 in 7 days. Some people move fast, some take a few weeks, some take a few months. Speed literally doesn't matter, but commitment does. What matters is that you finish in order. Each video builds on the last.
2
Fill in the prompts. Film the script.
Every video has a set of prompts. Your answers become your script. You don't need to memorize it. Just read it off your phone or use it as a guide.
3
Done is better than perfect.
Your first video doesn't have to be good. It just has to exist. You'll be amazed how much better Video 7 is than Video 1. That growth is the whole point.
4
The camera doesn't need a perfect you.
It needs the real you. Every video you film, messy, unpolished, uncertain, is proof that you showed up. That's the whole challenge.
